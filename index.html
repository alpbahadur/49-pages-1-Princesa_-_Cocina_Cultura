A Journey Through Time

Princesa - Cocina Cultura is more than a restaurant; it's a portal to Puerto Rico's rich culinary heritage. Nestled in the enchanting Paseo de la Princesa in Old San Juan, we are the only establishment dedicated to reviving recipes from 19th-century Puerto Rican cookbooks (1859-1950).

Our garden setting, with over 20 trees bonded to a historic wall, creates a magical atmosphere where history comes alive on every plate. We invite you to experience elevated Puerto Rican cuisine in a space that honors our island's past while celebrating its vibrant present.

The Rum Library

Explore one of the most extensive collections of Puerto Rican rums in the world. Our carefully curated library showcases the island's rich distilling heritage, from rare vintage bottles to contemporary craft expressions.

Whether you're a seasoned rum enthusiast or just beginning your journey, our knowledgeable staff will guide you through tastings and pairings that elevate your dining experience.

Finca La Zafra

Our commitment to authenticity extends beyond the kitchen to our own agroecological farm, Finca La Zafra. Here, we grow many of the fresh ingredients that grace your plate, cultivated using sustainable practices that honor the land.

By maintaining our own farm and partnering with local producers, we ensure that every dish reflects the true flavors of Puerto Rico while supporting our community and preserving our agricultural heritage.
